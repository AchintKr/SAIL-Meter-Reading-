About Bokaro Steel Limited

Bokaro Steel Limited, a flagship unit of Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL), was established in 1964 with the assistance from the Soviet Union. It has been a cornerstone of India's steel production industry, embodying technological advancement and self-sufficiency.

Bokaro Steel operates five blast furnaces and is currently undergoing a major modernization to increase its capacity to 12 million tonnes. Over the years, it has played a pivotal role in India's economic growth and industrial development.
