Foundation and Growth

Bokaro Steel Plant (BSL) is one of India's largest and oldest steel plants, established in collaboration with the Soviet Union in the early 1960s. It played a crucial role in the industrialization and economic development of India, particularly in the eastern region.

Technological Advancements

Bokaro Steel Plant has been at the forefront of technological advancements in the steel industry. It was one of the first plants in India to adopt the Basic Oxygen Furnace (BOF) technology, which significantly enhanced its production capabilities and quality of steel.

Integrated Township

The plant is not just a manufacturing hub but also a self-contained township. It has its own residential areas, educational institutions, hospitals, shopping complexes, and recreational facilities, making it a complete community in itself, designed to support its employees and their families.
